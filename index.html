Baklava

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making homemade baklava! This rich, flaky pastry is perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth with its layers of crispy phyllo, fragrant nuts, and luscious honey syrup. Follow this simple recipe to create delicious, melt-in-your-mouth baklava that everyone will love.

Ingredients

For the filling:

  • 2 cups mixed nuts (walnuts, pistachios, almonds), finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the phyllo layers:

  • 1 package (16 oz) phyllo dough, thawed
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the honey syrup:

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix the chopped nuts, sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Layer the phyllo sheets in the dish, brushing each sheet with melted butter as you go, stacking about 8 sheets.
  4. Spread a thin layer of the nut mixture over the layered sheets.
  5. Continue layering phyllo sheets (about 8 sheets per layer), buttering each sheet, then add more nut filling. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a top layer of about 8 sheets.
  6. Using a sharp knife, cut into diamond or square shapes before baking.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the baklava is golden brown and crisp.
  8. While baking, combine honey, water, sugar, lemon juice, and cinnamon stick in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, if using.
  9. Once baked, pour the hot syrup evenly over the hot baklava. Let it cool completely and absorb the syrup for at least 4 hours or overnight — the longer, the better!
